Visit Us at Sixteen Fourteen Ranch

Our ranch is located in the scenic countryside of Tyler, Texas, offering a perfect escape into the beauty of East Texas.

Address: 1614 Ranch Road, Tyler, TX 75701

Phone: (123) 456-7890

Email: info@1614ranch.com

Directions

If you're coming from Tyler, take Highway 69 South, and turn right onto Ranch Road. Continue for about 5 miles, and you'll find our ranch on the left.

Local Attractions

  • Explore the nearby Tyler State Park, known for its hiking trails and fishing spots.
  • Visit the Tyler Rose Garden, the nation's largest rose garden.
  • Don't miss the beautiful antique shops and art galleries in downtown Tyler.
