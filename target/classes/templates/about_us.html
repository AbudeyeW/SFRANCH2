About Us

Our Story

At Sixteen Fourteen Ranch, we're more than just a place. We're a family-owned venture rooted in love, nature, and the spirit of adventure.

Our Mission

With a deep connection to East Texas, we aim to share the beauty of our ranch and the wonders of nature. Our goal is to create unforgettable moments for all our visitors.

Family Heritage

Our name, Sixteen Fourteen, is inspired by 1 Corinthians 16:14 from the Bible - "Let all that you do be done in love." This verse reflects the heart and soul of our ranch, where love, care, and adventure come together.

Core Values

  • Foster a sense of community
  • Cherish nature and its blessings
  • Create memorable experiences
  • Share our love for East Texas
