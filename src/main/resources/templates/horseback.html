Horseback Riding at Sixteen Fourteen Ranch

At Sixteen Fourteen Ranch, we offer an exhilarating horseback riding experience that combines the beauty of the East Texas countryside with the charm of our ranch. Immerse yourself in nature as you embark on a trail ride adventure that will create lasting memories.
Activity Details:

Trail Options:

Choose from various scenic trails that cater to riders of all levels. Whether you're a novice or an experienced rider, we have trails suited for your skills.

Scenic Nature Trail

A leisurely ride through the scenic woodlands of East Texas. Ideal for beginners and families looking to connect with nature.
1hr

Sunset Serenity Trail

An evening trail ride as the sun sets over the Texan countryside. Perfect for couples or those seeking a romantic and tranquil experience.

Challenging Terrain Trail

Explore more rugged and challenging terrains for experienced riders. A thrilling adventure through varying landscapes.

Educational Heritage Trail

Learn about the local history and heritage as you ride through historical sites. Suitable for riders interested in the cultural aspects of the region.

Horse Selection

Our horses are well-trained and come in different sizes, allowing us to match you with the perfect companion based on your weight and riding experience.

Age Limit

Riders must be at least 12 years old to participate in horseback riding activities.

Weight Limit

To ensure the safety of our horses and riders, there's a maximum weight limit of 250 pounds for all participants. This limit includes all riding gear.

Riding Attire

We recommend wearing comfortable attire suitable for horseback riding. Closed-toe shoes or boots are mandatory. Helmets are available for those who prefer to use

Expert Guides

Our knowledgeable guides ensure your safety and comfort. They'll lead you through the trails, sharing interesting insights about the local flora and fauna.

Horse Care

At Sixteen Fourteen Ranch, we prioritize the welfare of our horses. Our commitment to equine well-being includes proper feeding, exercise, and regular veterinary care.

Safety First

Your safety is of the utmost importance to us. We provide guidelines and safety instructions before every ride to ensure you have a secure and enjoyable experience.

Reservations

To guarantee your spot, we recommend making a reservation in advance. Our team is here to accommodate your preferred date and time.

Restrictions

Pregnant individuals are advised not to participate in horseback riding activities. Riders with serious medical conditions or recent surgeries should consult with their healthcare provider before participating. Note: All riders are required to sign a waiver before embarking on a horseback riding adventure. Our staff will provide further details during your reservation.
