Your Privacy Matters

At Sixteen Fourteen Ranch, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal information while using our website and services.

Information Collected

We may collect personal information, including your name, email address, and phone number, when you contact us or make reservations. We also collect non-personal information through cookies for website analytics.

Use of Information

We use your personal information to communicate with you, process reservations, and improve our services. Non-personal information is used for website analytics and enhancing the user experience.

Data Security

We implement security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access. However, please be aware that no online data transmission or storage is 100% secure.

Third Parties

We do not share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes. However, we may use third-party booking services to process reservations. While these third parties are subject to their own privacy policies, we ensure they have security measures in place to protect your data.

Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our practices. We encourage you to review this policy periodically for any updates.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at privacy@sixteenfourteenranch.com.
